Delivery Information

We offer several delivery options to ensure your cake arrives fresh and on time:

  • Standard Delivery: Order at least 48 hours in advance for delivery on your specified date.
  • Rush Delivery: Need a cake sooner? Contact us for availability and additional fees.

Due to the perishable nature of our products, we do not accept returns. However, if you are not completely satisfied with your order, please contact us within 24 hours of receiving your cake and we will work to make it right.

About Us

Our Sweet Story

Sweet Delights began in 2010 as a small family-owned bakery with a passion for creating delicious, handcrafted cakes. What started as a hobby in founder Maria Garcia's kitchen has grown into a beloved bakery known for premium quality and exceptional designs.

Over the years, we've maintained our commitment to using only the finest ingredients and traditional baking methods, while continuously innovating with new flavors and designs. Every cake we create is a labor of love, made to bring joy to your special occasions.

Today, our team of talented pastry chefs continues to push the boundaries of cake design, creating edible works of art that not only look stunning but taste amazing too. We're proud to be part of thousands of celebrations each year, from intimate birthdays to grand weddings.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to our most commonly asked questions.

How far in advance should I order a cake?

For standard cakes, we recommend ordering at least 48 hours in advance. For custom or wedding cakes, we suggest placing your order 2-4 weeks ahead to ensure availability.

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Yes! We offer vegan, gluten-free, and nut-free options. Please let us know about any allergies or dietary requirements when placing your order.

What is your delivery area?

We deliver throughout the New York City area, including Manhattan, Brooklyn, Queens, and the Bronx. Additional delivery fees may apply depending on the location.

Can I request a cake tasting?

Absolutely! We offer cake tastings by appointment. This is particularly recommended for wedding cakes or large event orders. Please contact us to schedule a tasting session.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. For orders over $500, we require a 50% deposit at the time of ordering.
